Synopsis

Story and emotional framing live together here rather than getting scattered.

Three best friends go across America to pursue the dream...

The documentary follows three best friends through hotel suites, late sets, long drives, and the strange optimism it takes to keep betting on yourself.

It is a character-first road film: funny, uncertain, and emotionally grounded instead of over-explained.

Themes
  • Creative ambition
  • Friendship under stress
  • The comedy grind as lived experience
